All About Yhn Rb

Music HomePage

Yhn Rb is an artist out of NewHaven, CT that recently started rapping in 2022 and is now starting to get a buzz he started off making music for fun and then eventually kept going once he seen he had a flow and dropped his first song " Frontline " from there he seen the reaction people gave him from his music so he decided to keep going. Rb says that growing up in New Haven was hard and he seen alot coming from his environment. He wants to use his image as a rapper to show the younger generation that there is hope no matter where you are from. He wants to change the narrative that you dont have to be in the streets to be something in your community. He wants to teach that you can do or be something positive in your community without all the negativity.
